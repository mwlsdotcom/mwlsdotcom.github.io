About Mike Wills Learning Services

Home

Mike Wills Learning Services facilitates individual and organisational learning.

I also run the Learning Pages project which is an advert-free site that provides free, online resources for learners, trainers and educators. It has been motivated by the desire that learning should not be lost.

As well as the usual resources you would expect on a site that is dedicated to educating, training and learning, you will also find resources that reflect my personal learning journey.

If you would like to support this work with donations, microdonations or by taking out a monthly subscription, please go to the Learning Pages Donation Page.

During the pandemic, I realised how important books are for leisure, mental health and learning. This inspired me to create an online bookshop - Lyme Bay Books - with a wide range of categories.

Learning Pages and Lyme Bay Books are trading names of Mike Wills Learning Services.
